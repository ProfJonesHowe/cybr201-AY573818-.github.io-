AC Milan Soccer Club

AC Milan, officially called Associazione Calcio Milan, is one of the most famous soccer clubs in the world. Founded in 1899 in Milan, Italy, the team is known for its red and black stripes and its long tradition of success. Over the years, AC Milan has won many Serie A titles and Champions League trophies, and it plays home matches at the iconic San Siro stadium. The club is loved worldwide for its history, passionate fans, and legendary players.
